Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a train ticket?

Simply search for trains by entering source, destination, and date. Select your preferred train, choose seats, enter passenger details, and proceed to payment.

How can I check my PNR status?

Go to the PNR Status page and enter your 10-digit PNR number to get real-time booking status and journey details.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation charges vary based on the time of cancellation. Cancel at least 4 hours before departure for minimal charges.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are processed automatically to your original payment method within 7-10 business days after cancellation.

What documents do I need to carry?

Carry a valid government-issued photo ID proof mentioned during booking and your e-ticket (printed or digital).
